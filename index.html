2.1 Moving the Sprite


Movement is the most basic function in any game. Sprites must move to animate the game and act like real things. For example, the ball and the bar move in a ping-pong game. In the Mario game, Mario moves when arrow keys are pressed on the keyboard, while the enemy sprite moves on its own.
